Who we are

There are so many animals day in & day out that are subjected to cruelty, road rage or just medical issues. TalesOFTales works towards helping such animals either on site or by admitting them into our facility.

TalesOfTales strongly believes in acting against animal cruelty and helping thousands to live longer and happier. We encourage all of you to be a part of it either by helping animals around you or by donating money so we could work and reach to many more in need
